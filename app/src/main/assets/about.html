It began with the forging of the Great Rings.

Three were given to the Elves,

immortal, wisest and fairest of all beings.

Seven to the Dwarf-Lords,

great miners and craftsmen of the mountain halls.

And nine, nine rings were

gifted to the race of Men,

who above all else desire power.

For within these rings was bound the strength

and the will to govern each race.

But they were all of them deceived,

for another ring was made.

Deep in the land of Mordor, in the Fires of Mount

Doom, the Dark Lord Sauron forged a master ring,

and into this ring he poured his cruelty,

his malice and his will to dominate all life.
